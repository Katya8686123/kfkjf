Saint Petersburg

St. Petersburg is one of the most unique and exciting cities in Russia. One of its most interesting attractions is the Grand Layout Russia project. This amazing scale model is not just a model, but a real cultural and historical experience that takes you to the heart of the country.

Regardless of whether you are a lover of history, architecture or just looking for a unique experience, Grand Layout Russia will offer you something more than just a guided tour. Here you can immerse yourself in the atmosphere of Russian cities, feel their uniqueness and unsurpassed diversity. About a hundred people worked on the creation of the layout for five years. First, a load-bearing sub-package frame was installed, the foundations of railways and highways were laid. Next, wooden ribs were installed throughout the layout, a metal mesh was stretched along their upper edge and a layer of gypsum was applied (about 15 tons were used in total), from which the final outlines of the desired landscape were created. It took about a month of work by one specialist to design a square meter of the layout surface. The museum welcomed its first visitors in April 2011. For the next 14 months, the museum operated in a test mode, receiving guests only on weekends. The official opening took place on June 8, 2012.
